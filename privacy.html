Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. It is AI Captionist's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information like your name, email, or phone number unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., emailing support). All video processing happens securely.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., crash reports, feature usage statistics) to help us improve the app.

2. AI Processing

Videos uploaded for captioning are processed securely. We do not store your videos on our servers after processing is complete. They are used solely for the purpose of generating captions.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for specific functions:

  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions.
  • OpenAI: For caption generation intelligence.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@aicaptionist.com.
